About Jutta Pasal

Nepal's premier destination for authentic, stylish, and comfortable footwear since 2020.

Our Story

Jutta Pasal was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to quality footwear at fair prices. Founded in 2020 in the heart of Kathmandu, we started as a small shop with a big dream.

Today, we've grown into one of Nepal's most trusted online shoe retailers, serving thousands of happy customers across all seven provinces. Our commitment to authenticity, quality, and customer satisfaction remains at the core of everything we do.

We work directly with authorized distributors and brands to ensure every pair of shoes we sell is 100% genuine. Whether you're looking for the latest sneakers, classic formals, or comfortable casuals, Jutta Pasal has got you covered.
